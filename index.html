About Me

Hello! My name is Jenika Isenhart (she/her) and I am a Web Developer with a dental background residing in Western Washington. I have been incredibly lucky to grow up in the PNW with access to such beautiful landscapes and outdoor actvities. I am extremly grateful to live somehwere I can ski, fish, hike and so much more! Now about Web Development.... I've spent the last 6 years of my life in the dental field working as a Dental Assistant. Over the years I had grown frustrated over how many dental providers had websites that were lacking the basic fundamentals of a well done site. I decided to take matters into my own hands and persue a career in web development to better equipt many dental providers and business owners with the website they need to cater to our ever growing tech savy generation.
